Core Concepts: The Study of Carbon

Organic chemistry is a subdiscipline within chemistry involving the scientific study of the structure, properties, and reactions of organic compounds and organic materials. Study of structure determines their structural formula...

The range of chemicals studied in organic chemistry includes hydrocarbons as well as compounds based on carbon, but also containing other elements, especially oxygen, nitrogen, sulfur, phosphorus...

Organic compounds form the basis of all earthly life... They form the basis of, or are constituents of, many commercial products including:

  • Pharmaceuticals
  • Petrochemicals and agrichemicals
  • Plastics, fuels, and explosives
