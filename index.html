All About Tigers

Tigers are the largest members of the cat family and are known for their beautiful striped coats. They are found mainly in Asia and are apex predators, playing a crucial role in maintaining the balance of their ecosystems.

Tigers are solitary and territorial animals. They hunt primarily at night and rely on their keen senses and powerful build to catch prey. Unfortunately, tiger populations are endangered due to habitat loss and poaching.
