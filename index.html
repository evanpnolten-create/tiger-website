Bengal Tiger

The Bengal tiger is found mainly in India and Bangladesh. It is the most numerous tiger subspecies, known for its vibrant orange coat with black stripes.

Conservation Status: Endangered

Siberian Tiger

The Siberian tiger inhabits the Russian Far East. It is the largest tiger subspecies and has a thicker, paler coat to survive cold winters.

Conservation Status: Endangered

Sumatran Tiger

Native to the Indonesian island of Sumatra, this tiger is smaller and darker with thicker stripes, adapted to dense tropical forests.

Conservation Status: Critically Endangered
