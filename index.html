All About Tigers

Tigers are the largest members of the cat family and are known for their beautiful striped coats. They are apex predators and play a crucial role in maintaining ecosystem balance.

Tigers are mostly found in diverse habitats ranging from tropical forests, mangroves, grasslands to savannas across Asia. They require dense vegetation and water sources to thrive.

Tigers are solitary animals and primarily hunt at night. They rely on stealth and power to hunt prey. Their magnificent stripes serve as camouflage in dense forests.

Unfortunately, they face threats like habitat loss and poaching, leading to declining populations.
